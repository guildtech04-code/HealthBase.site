To develop a comprehensive patient information and consultation management module that securely organizes patient data, medical histories, and doctor consultation records to enhance clinical decision-making and accuracy.

1.1 Integrate patient registration, health record updates, and consultation documentation into a unified digital workflow.

1.2 Ensure role-based access and data privacy compliance in all patient-related transactions.

To design and implement a SMART scheduling system that automates appointment management using patient priority, doctor availability, and predicted health risk factors.

2.1 Optimize resource allocation through adaptive scheduling algorithms that minimize waiting time and reschedule based on urgency.

2.2 Provide automated reminders and notifications for both patients and staff to enhance coordination and reduce missed appointments.
